Plasma Separators are used to separate plasma from blood cells, while Plasma Component Separators separate plasma components for specific treatments. Immunoadsorption Columns remove specific antibodies from the blood, Plasma Perfusion Columns separate plasma from whole blood, and Hemoperfusion Columns remove toxins from the blood. Each type serves a different purpose in the continuous flow apheresis process, catering to specific medical needs.</p></p>
