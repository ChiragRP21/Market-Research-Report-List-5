<p><p>Denatured Alcohol is classified into two main types: Specially Denatured Alcohol (SDA) and Completely Denatured Alcohol (CDA). SDA is a type of alcohol that has been specifically denatured according to a formula approved by the government for various industrial applications. CDA, on the other hand, is completely denatured with substances that render it unfit for consumption and is typically used in industrial processes where the presence of alcohol is necessary but its consumption is to be prevented.</p></p>
