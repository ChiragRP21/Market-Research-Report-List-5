<p><p>The automotive liftgate market consists of two main types: conventional power liftgate and hands-free power liftgate. Conventional power liftgate systems operate with the use of buttons or switches to open and close the liftgate. Hands-free power liftgate systems, on the other hand, use sensors or foot-activated technology to automatically open or close the liftgate without requiring physical contact with the vehicle. Both types offer convenience and ease of use for accessing the cargo area of a vehicle.</p></p>
