<p><p>Oilfield corrosion and scale inhibitor market types include inorganic phosphate, organophosphorus compounds, polymer scale inhibitor, and others. Inorganic phosphates work by forming a protective layer on metal surfaces, while organophosphorus compounds inhibit scale formation by interfering with crystal growth. Polymer scale inhibitors prevent scale formation by dispersing scale-forming ions in the water. Other inhibitors may include surfactants, biocides, and oxygen scavengers, each providing unique benefits in preventing corrosion and scale build-up in oilfield operations.</p></p>
