<p><p>Recombinant Antibody Market Types include Chimeric Antibodies, which are composed of mouse variable regions and human constant regions. Humanized Antibodies have most of the mouse components replaced with human sequences. Full Human Antibodies are entirely composed of human sequences. Single Chain Antibodies consist of only one chain of variable regions. Bispecific Antibodies can bind to two different targets simultaneously. These different types of antibodies offer unique characteristics and advantages for various therapeutic applications in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
