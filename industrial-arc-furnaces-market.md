<p><p>Industrial Arc Furnaces are used for melting and refining metals in various industries. There are two main types of Industrial Arc Furnaces - AC-EAF and DC-EAF. AC-EAF uses alternating current for the heating process, while DC-EAF uses direct current. AC-EAFs are known for their lower operating costs and higher energy efficiency, while DC-EAFs offer better control over the melting process and are capable of melting a wider range of materials. Both types play a crucial role in the metalworking industry.</p></p>
