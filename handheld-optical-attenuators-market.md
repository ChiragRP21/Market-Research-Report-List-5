<p><p>Handheld optical attenuators are devices used to reduce the power of optical signals in fiber optic communication systems. They are available in two main types: single mode and multimode. Single mode attenuators are designed for use with single mode fiber optic cables, which are used for long distance communication. Multimode attenuators, on the other hand, are used with multimode fiber optic cables, typically for shorter distance and lower bandwidth applications. Both types of attenuators help to ensure proper signal strength and quality in optical communication networks.</p></p>
