<p><p>Optical Fiber Market Types consist of Single Mode Optical Fiber and Multimode Optical Fiber. Single Mode Optical Fiber is designed for long-distance transmission with a narrow core diameter, allowing only one mode of light to travel through. Multimode Optical Fiber, on the other hand, has a larger core diameter which enables multiple modes of light to travel through, making it suitable for shorter distances. Both market types play a crucial role in the telecommunications industry by providing high-speed and reliable data transmission.</p></p>
