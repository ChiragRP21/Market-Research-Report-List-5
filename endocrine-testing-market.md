Two common types of endocrine testing markets are tandem mass spectrometry and immunoassay. Tandem mass spectrometry is a highly sensitive technique that can accurately measure hormone levels in blood or urine samples. Both methods play a crucial role in diagnosing endocrine disorders and monitoring hormone levels for various medical purposes.</p></p>
