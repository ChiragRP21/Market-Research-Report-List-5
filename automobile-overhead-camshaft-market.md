<p><p>The Automobile Overhead Camshaft market primarily consists of three types - Single Overhead Camshaft (SOHC), Dual Overhead Camshaft (DOHC), and other market variations. SOHC engines have one camshaft per cylinder head, limiting the number of valves that can be operated. DOHC engines have two camshafts per cylinder head, allowing for better control of valve timing and increased performance. Other market variations may include variations in camshaft placement, such as Triple overhead camshafts or cam-in-block designs. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on specific performance needs.</p></p>
