<p><p>Surgeon cooling vests come in different types to help medical professionals maintain a comfortable body temperature during long surgical procedures. Circulatory powered cooling vests use a pump to circulate chilled water throughout the vest. Cold pack cooling vests contain removable gel packs that can be frozen and inserted into the vest. Phase change cooling vests utilize materials that absorb heat as they change from a solid to a liquid state. Other types of cooling vests may use different technologies or materials to achieve the same goal of keeping surgeons cool and focused during operations.</p></p>
