<p><p>Natural gas-fired electricity generation can be categorized into two main market types: Combined Cycle Gas Turbine (CCGT) and Open Cycle Gas Turbine (OCGT). </p><p>CCGT plants are more efficient, utilizing waste heat to generate additional power, making them the preferred choice for large-scale, continuous electricity generation. They are more cost-effective and environmentally friendly compared to OCGT plants, which are typically used for peaking power during periods of high demand. OCGT plants are known for their flexibility and ability to quickly ramp up power output when needed.</p></p>
