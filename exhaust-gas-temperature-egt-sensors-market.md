<p><p>Exhaust Gas Temperature (EGT) Sensors serve the purpose of monitoring the temperature of the exhaust gas in vehicles. There are two main types of EGT sensors, which are PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) Sensors and NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) Sensors. PTC sensors exhibit an increase in electrical resistance with an increase in temperature, while NTC sensors show a decrease in resistance with increasing temperature. Both types of sensors are vital in ensuring the efficient operation of the engine and emissions control systems in vehicles.</p></p>
