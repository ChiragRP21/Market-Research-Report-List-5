<p><strong>The Brake Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller Brake Testers</li><li>Plate Brake Testers</li><li>Decelerometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brake testers are used to evaluate the braking performance of vehicles. Roller brake testers measure brake force by rolling the vehicle's wheels over rotating drums. Plate brake testers use a flat plate to calculate braking force. Decelerometers measure the vehicle's speed reduction when braking. Roller brake testers are commonly used in garages and inspection centers, while plate brake testers are preferred in workshops. Decelerometers are portable and user-friendly, making them suitable for on-road brake testing. Each type of brake tester caters to specific needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
