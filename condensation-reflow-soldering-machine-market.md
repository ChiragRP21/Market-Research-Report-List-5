<p><p>Condensation reflow soldering machines are available in two main market types: manual and automatic. Manual machines require operators to manually load and unload the PCBs, apply solder paste, and control the soldering process. Automatic machines, on the other hand, feature automated processes for loading and unloading PCBs, applying solder paste, and controlling the soldering process. Automatic machines offer higher productivity and consistency in solder joint quality compared to manual machines but come at a higher cost.</p></p>
