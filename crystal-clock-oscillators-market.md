<p><ul><li>SMD/SMT</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crystal Clock Oscillators are available in two main types - SMD/SMT and Through Hole. SMD/SMT (Surface Mount Device/Technology) crystal clock oscillators are smaller in size and are designed for surface mounting on printed circuit boards. They are ideal for compact electronic devices. On the other hand, Through Hole crystal clock oscillators are larger in size and have leads that are inserted into holes on the PCB for soldering. They are suitable for applications where reliability and durability are crucial.</p></p>
