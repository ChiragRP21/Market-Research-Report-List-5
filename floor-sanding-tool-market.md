<p><p>Floor sanding tools can be categorized into two main types: dry polishing and wet polishing. Dry polishing involves using sanding tools without any water or liquid, resulting in a dustier environment but faster cutting speed. On the other hand, wet polishing uses water or liquid to cool the tools and reduce dust, resulting in a cleaner work area but slower cutting speed. Both methods have their advantages and are suitable for different types of projects and environments.</p></p>
