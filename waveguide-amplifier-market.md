<p><p>Waveguide amplifiers are categorized into different types based on their applications and functionalities. Waveguide power amplifiers are designed to increase the power level of electromagnetic signals passing through the waveguide. On the other hand, waveguide low-noise amplifiers are intended to amplify weak signals while minimizing added noise. Other types of waveguide amplifiers may include broadband amplifiers, which are capable of amplifying signals across a wide frequency range, and dual-mode amplifiers, which can amplify signals in multiple modes simultaneously.</p></p>
