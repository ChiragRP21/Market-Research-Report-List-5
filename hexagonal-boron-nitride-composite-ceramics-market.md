<p><p>Hexagonal Boron Nitride Composite Ceramics refers to materials that combine the properties of hexagonal boron nitride with other materials like graphite or diamond structures. Graphite Structure Hexagonal Boron Nitride Composite Ceramics are known for their high thermal conductivity and lubricity, making them ideal for applications in high-temperature environments. On the other hand, Diamond Structure Hexagonal Boron Nitride Composite Ceramics exhibit exceptional hardness and wear resistance, making them suitable for cutting tools and abrasive applications. Both types of composite ceramics offer unique properties that cater to different industrial needs.</p></p>
