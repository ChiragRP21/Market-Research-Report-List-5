<p><strong>The Nonwoven Fabrics for Protective Masks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbond Nonwoven</li><li>Meltblown Nonwoven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spunbond nonwoven fabrics are made by bonding together continuous filaments of polypropylene. They provide high strength, durability, and breathability, making them ideal for use in protective masks. Meltblown nonwoven fabrics, on the other hand, are made by extruding melted polymer fibers onto a conveyor belt. They offer excellent filtration properties, capturing even the smallest particles. Both spunbond and meltblown nonwoven fabrics are essential components in the production of high-quality protective masks, ensuring efficiency and protection for the wearer.</p></p>
