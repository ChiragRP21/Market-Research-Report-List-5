<p><ul><li>Cellulose-Based Membranes</li><li>Thin Film Composite Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reverse Osmosis Membrane Market consists of two main types- Cellulose-Based Membranes and Thin Film Composite Membranes. Cellulose-Based Membranes are made from natural cellulose material, offering good contaminant removal capabilities. On the other hand, Thin Film Composite Membranes consist of a thin polyamide film on top of a porous support layer, providing superior rejection properties and high efficiency. These membranes are essential components in reverse osmosis systems used for water purification, desalination, and other industrial processes.</p></p>
