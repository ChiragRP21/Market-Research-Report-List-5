<p><p>Car Exhaust Heat Recovery Systems are designed to capture and harness the waste heat generated by a vehicle's exhaust gases. The three main types of systems in the market are Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) systems, Rankine Cycle Systems, and other innovative technologies. EGHR systems use heat exchangers to transfer exhaust heat to a working fluid, which can then be used to power ancillary systems. Rankine Cycle Systems use a closed-loop system to convert heat into mechanical energy. Other emerging technologies include thermoelectric and turbogenerators for more efficient heat recovery.</p></p>
