<p><p>Civil Aircraft Weighing Equipment market includes different types such as Platform System and Jack Weigh System. Platform System involves placing the aircraft on a set of weighing platforms to determine its weight distribution accurately. Jack Weigh System, on the other hand, uses hydraulic jacks to raise specific points of the aircraft to measure its weight. Both systems are crucial for ensuring the safety and efficiency of civil aircraft operations by providing accurate weight measurements for maintenance, loading, and fuel calculations.</p></p>
