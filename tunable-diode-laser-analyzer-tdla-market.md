<p><p>Tunable Diode Laser Analyzer (TDLA) is used in two main market types: In-Situ TDLA and Extractive TDLA. In-Situ TDLA systems measure gas concentrations directly in the process stream, providing real-time data without the need for sample extraction. Extractive TDLA systems extract a sample from the process stream and analyze it offline, offering more flexibility in terms of sample preparation and analysis. Both types of TDLA systems have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
