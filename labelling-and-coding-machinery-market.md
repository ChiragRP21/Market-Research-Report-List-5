<p><strong>The Labelling and Coding Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-adhesive or Pressure Sensitive Labellers</li><li>Shrink Sleeve Labellers</li><li>Glue Based Labellers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Labelling and coding machinery market types include self-adhesive or pressure sensitive labellers, which apply labels using adhesive, shrink sleeve labellers, which use heat to shrink labels to bottles or containers, and glue-based labellers, which use glue to secure labels. Self-adhesive labellers are popular for their ease of use and versatility, while shrink sleeve labellers are ideal for contoured or irregularly shaped containers. Glue-based labellers provide a strong bond for labels but require more maintenance. Each type of labeller caters to different needs in packaging and labeling products.</p></p>
