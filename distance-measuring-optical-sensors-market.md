<p><p>Distance measuring optical sensors market types include laser and LED sensors. Laser sensors use laser technology to accurately measure distances by emitting a laser beam and calculating the time it takes for the beam to reflect back. LED sensors, on the other hand, use light emitting diodes to measure distances by emitting light and detecting the reflection of this light. Both types of sensors are commonly used in various applications such as industrial automation, robotics, and automotive systems for precise distance measurement.</p></p>
