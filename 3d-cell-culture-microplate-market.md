<p><strong>The 3D Cell Culture Microplate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scaffold-based</li><li>Scaffold-free</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scaffold-based 3D cell culture microplates provide a structure for cells to adhere to, offering a more realistic environment for cell growth and interaction. This type of microplate mimics the extracellular matrix, allowing for better cell differentiation and function. On the other hand, scaffold-free 3D cell culture microplates do not use any structure to support cell growth, relying on cell-cell interactions for aggregation and organization. This type of microplate allows for more natural cell behavior and has applications in drug screening and tissue engineering.</p></p>
