<p><p>Optical shutters are widely used in various applications such as microscopy, spectroscopy, and laser systems. The three main types of optical shutters are vibration-free optical shutters, super-low vibration optical shutters, and standard optical shutters. Vibration-free optical shutters are designed to minimize vibrations during operation, ensuring high precision in sensitive applications. Super-low vibration optical shutters offer improved stability compared to standard shutters, making them suitable for critical applications. Standard optical shutters are commonly used in general optical setups where strict vibration control is not required.</p></p>
