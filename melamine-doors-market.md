<p><p>Melamine doors are commonly found with two types of core materials: MDF core and particleboard core. MDF core doors are made with medium-density fiberboard, which provides a denser and more durable structure, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Particleboard core doors are made from compressed wood chips, offering a more cost-effective option without compromising on quality. Both types of doors are finished with a melamine coating, providing a smooth and durable surface that is easy to maintain and resistant to scratches and stains.</p></p>
