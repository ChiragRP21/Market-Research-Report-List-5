<p><p>Viscosity Reducing Films are categorized into two main types based on their properties and applications. UV Viscosity Reducing Films are designed to decrease the viscosity of materials when exposed to ultraviolet light, making it easier to work with these substances. On the other hand, Thermal Viscosity Reducing Films operate by reducing viscosity through the application of heat, enabling better flow and maneuverability of the materials being treated. Both types of films offer solutions for different industries and processes requiring viscosity reduction.</p></p>
