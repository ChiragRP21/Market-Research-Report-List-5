<p><strong>The Cationic Light Cured Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oligomers</li><li>Monomers</li><li>Photoinitiators</li><li>Additives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cationic light-cured resin market consists of four main types: oligomers, monomers, photoinitiators, and additives. Oligomers are high-molecular-weight molecules that serve as the backbone of the resin system. Monomers are lower molecular weight molecules that help in the cross-linking process. Photoinitiators are compounds that initiate the curing reaction when exposed to light. Additives are ingredients that enhance the performance and properties of the resin. Each of these components plays a crucial role in the formulation and curing process of cationic light-cured resins.</p></p>
