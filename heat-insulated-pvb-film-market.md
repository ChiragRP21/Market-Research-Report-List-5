<p><strong>The Heat Insulated PVB Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endothermic Film</li><li>Reflective Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endothermic Film is a type of heat insulated PVB film that absorbs and dissipates heat, preventing it from transferring to the other side. This increased thermal performance helps to protect buildings and vehicles from heat transfer. Reflective Film, on the other hand, reflects heat away from the surface, providing an additional layer of insulation and reducing energy costs. Both types of heat insulated PVB films offer effective solutions for temperature control and energy efficiency in a variety of applications.</p></p>
