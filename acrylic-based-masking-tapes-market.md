<p><p>Acrylic-based masking tapes are available in two types: single side and double side. Single side masking tapes are adhesive tapes with acrylic adhesive on only one side, commonly used for painting and surface protection in various industries. On the other hand, double side masking tapes have acrylic adhesive on both sides, allowing them to adhere to two surfaces simultaneously. Double side tapes are used for bonding two surfaces together temporarily or permanently. Both types offer ease of use and precise application for different masking needs.</p></p>
