<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Phenolic Resin</li><li>Thermosetting Phenolic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phenolic resins are categorized into two main types: thermoplastic phenolic resin and thermosetting phenolic resin. Thermoplastic phenolic resins are capable of being melted and reformed multiple times, making them suitable for applications like adhesives and coatings. On the other hand, thermosetting phenolic resins undergo a permanent chemical change when heated, making them ideal for durable applications such as automotive components and electrical insulation. Both types of phenolic resins play essential roles in various industrial sectors due to their unique properties and versatility.</p></p>
