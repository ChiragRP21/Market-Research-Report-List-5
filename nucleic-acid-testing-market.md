<p><strong>The Nucleic Acid Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transcription-mediated Amplification (TMA)</li><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)</li><li>Ligase Chain Reaction (LCR)</li><li>Whole Genome Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nucleic Acid Testing market offers various types of technologies for detecting genetic material in samples. Transcription-mediated Amplification (TMA) method involves amplifying RNA to detect viruses like HIV. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) involves replicating DNA sequences to diagnose diseases. Ligase Chain Reaction (LCR) helps in identifying specific DNA strands by linking them together. Whole Genome Sequencing market involves mapping the entire DNA sequence of an organism. These technologies play a crucial role in diagnosing diseases and understanding genetic information.</p></p>
