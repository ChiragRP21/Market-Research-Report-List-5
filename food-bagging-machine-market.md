<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food bagging machines can be categorized into two main types: Fully automatic and semi-automatic. </p><p>Fully automatic machines are designed to operate without the need for human intervention, carrying out the entire bagging process from start to finish. These machines are ideal for large-scale production facilities where efficiency and speed are crucial.</p><p>On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of human involvement, such as manually placing the food products into the bags or sealing them. These machines are more suitable for smaller production settings or where flexibility is required in the bagging process.</p></p>
