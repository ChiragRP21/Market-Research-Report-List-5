<p><p>Remote support software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based remote support software operates on a subscription basis, where the software is hosted and managed by a third-party provider. This allows for easy accessibility and scalability, making it a popular choice for businesses looking for flexibility and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premises remote support software is installed and operated on the company's own servers, giving businesses full control over security and customization, but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment.</p></p>
