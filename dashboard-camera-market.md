<p><p>Dashboard cameras, also known as dashcams, come in two main types: single channel dashcams and multi-channel dashcams. Single channel dashcams capture video footage from a single perspective, usually from the front windshield of the vehicle. They are ideal for personal use and recording accidents or incidents on the road. On the other hand, multi-channel dashcams can capture footage from multiple angles, such as front and rear views, or even inside the vehicle. They are commonly used by commercial drivers or fleet operators for enhanced surveillance and security purposes.</p></p>
