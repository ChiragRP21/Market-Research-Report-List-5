<p><p>Automatic washing machines are divided into two main market types: front-loading washing machines and top-loading washing machines with agitators. Front-loading washing machines are typically more energy efficient and use less water, making them popular among eco-conscious consumers. They also have higher spin speeds, resulting in faster drying times. On the other hand, top-loading washing machines with agitators are generally more affordable and have shorter wash cycles. They are preferred by those looking for a budget-friendly option with simple operation.</p></p>
