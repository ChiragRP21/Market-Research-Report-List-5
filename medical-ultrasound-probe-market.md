<p><strong>The Medical Ultrasound Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Type</li><li>Convex Type</li><li>Phased Array Type</li><li>Endocavitary Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical ultrasound probes come in various types to cater to different imaging needs. Linear type probes provide high resolution for superficial structures, while convex type probes are ideal for abdominal and obstetric imaging. Phased array probes are used for cardiac and vascular imaging due to their ability to produce detailed images at different depths. Endocavitary probes are designed for internal imaging, such as transvaginal or transrectal scans. Other types of probes include specialty probes for specific applications like intraoperative or intracavity imaging.</p></p>
