<p><p>Position and Proximity Sensors market is categorized into contact sensors and non-contact sensors. Contact sensors require physical contact with the object to detect its position or proximity. These sensors are commonly used in industrial machinery. On the other hand, non-contact sensors do not require physical contact and use technologies like ultrasonic, infrared, or magnetic fields to detect position or proximity. Non-contact sensors are preferred in applications where contact may cause damage or contamination. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries for automation and control purposes.</p></p>
