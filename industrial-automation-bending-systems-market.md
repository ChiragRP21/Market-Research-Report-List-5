<p><p>Industrial Automation Bending Systems include Air Bending and Bottom Bending market types. Air Bending is a versatile and popular method that uses press brakes to bend the metal sheet by applying force on the top tool. This method allows for varying bend angles without changing tooling. On the other hand, Bottom Bending involves pressing the sheet metal against a die and bending it using a hydraulic press. This method is commonly used for more complex and precise bending applications.</p></p>
