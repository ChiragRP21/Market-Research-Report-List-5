<p><ul><li>Ion-Assisted Deposition (IAD)</li><li>Electron Beam Evaporation</li><li>Sputtering</li><li>Vacuum Deposition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lens coatings are crucial in enhancing optical performance and durability of lenses. Different methods are used for lens coating such as Ion-Assisted Deposition (IAD), Electron Beam Evaporation, Sputtering, and Vacuum Deposition. IAD involves a plasma source to ionize particles for improved coating adherence. Electron Beam Evaporation uses high-energy electron beam for precise coating application. Sputtering involves bombarding the target material with high-energy particles for deposition. Vacuum Deposition creates a vacuum environment for coating process, ensuring high quality and uniformity.</p></p>
