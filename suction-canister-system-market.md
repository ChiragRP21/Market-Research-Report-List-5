<p><p>A Suction Canister System is a medical device used to collect and contain bodily fluids and tissue during surgical procedures or medical treatments that produce significant amounts of liquid waste. The canister collects the waste material, while the suction source creates negative pressure to remove the fluids from the patient.</p><p>The Suction Canister System Market is seeing steady growth, with a projected CAGR of 6.9% during the forecast period.
