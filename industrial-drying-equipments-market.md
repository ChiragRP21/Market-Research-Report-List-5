<p><p>Industrial drying equipments market includes various types such as rotary dryers, dispersion dryers, fluid bed dryers, belt dryers, and others. Rotary dryers use rotating drums to dry materials evenly. Dispersion dryers utilize high-speed dispersion blades to efficiently dry materials. Fluid bed dryers use a bed of particles to dry materials by passing air through them. Belt dryers use a continuous looped belt to dry materials as they pass through a heated chamber. Other types of drying equipments include freeze dryers, drum dryers, and tray dryers.</p></p>
