<p><p>Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension (PAH) treatment market consists of various types of medications including vasodilators, Phosphodiesterase 5 (PDE 5) Inhibitors, Endothelin Receptor Antagonists (ERA), and Soluble Guanylate Cyclase (SGC) Stimulators. Vasodilators help relax and widen blood vessels, PDE 5 inhibitors work by increasing blood flow in the lungs, ERAs block a hormone that causes blood vessels to narrow, and SGC stimulators help increase production of a molecule that relaxes blood vessels. These medications are used to manage symptoms and improve the quality of life for PAH patients.</p></p>
