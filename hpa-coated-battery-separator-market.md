<p><p>HPA coated battery separators are used in both wet diaphragm and dry diaphragm markets. In wet diaphragm market, the separator is soaked in electrolyte solution while in dry diaphragm market, the separator is used without any liquid electrolyte. Wet diaphragm separators are commonly used in lead-acid batteries for automotive applications, while dry diaphragm separators are used in lithium-ion batteries for electronic devices. Both types of separators help enhance battery performance and longevity by preventing short circuits and improving ion conductivity.</p></p>
