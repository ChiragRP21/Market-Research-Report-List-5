<p><p>Dual-arm robots are categorized into two main types based on their arm structure: Transversely Articulated and Longitudinal Articulated. Transversely Articulated robots have arms that move in a horizontal direction, making them ideal for tasks requiring flexibility and dexterity in tight spaces. On the other hand, Longitudinal Articulated robots have arms that move in a vertical direction, allowing for greater reach and precision in tasks that require a larger workspace. These two types cater to different applications and industries based on their unique arm movements and capabilities.</p></p>
