<p><strong>The Compression Stockings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gradient Socks</li><li>Anti-Embolism Socks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compression stockings are divided into two main types: Gradient socks and Anti-Embolism socks. Gradient socks apply graduated pressure to the legs, highest at the ankle and decreasing towards the top, to help improve circulation and reduce swelling. These are commonly used for conditions like varicose veins and mild edema. On the other hand, Anti-Embolism socks are designed to prevent blood clots in patients who are immobile, such as those recovering from surgery. They apply uniform pressure to the entire leg to promote blood flow and reduce the risk of clot formation.</p></p>
