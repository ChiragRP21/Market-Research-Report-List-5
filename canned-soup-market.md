<p><p>Canned soup market is divided into two main types: condensed soup and ready-to-eat soup. Ready-to-eat soup, on the other hand, is fully prepared and can be heated and consumed straight from the can. Both types of soups cater to different consumer needs and preferences, with condensed soup being popular for its longer shelf life and convenience, while ready-to-eat soup provides quick and easy meal solutions.</p></p>
