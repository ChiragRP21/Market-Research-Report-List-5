Flake ice maker machines produce soft, small ice flakes that are ideal for seafood displays or medical applications. Nugget ice maker machines produce small, chewable ice nuggets that are popular in healthcare facilities and restaurants for drinks. Each market type caters to specific needs and preferences in the commercial ice industry.</p></p>
