<p><p>Soluble fertilizers can be categorized into different types based on their composition and nutrient content. These include NPK Water-Soluble fertilizers, which contain the essential nutrients nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium in readily available form. Humic Acid Water-Soluble fertilizers contain humic acids that improve soil structure and nutrient uptake. Amino Acid Water-Soluble fertilizers provide plants with essential amino acids for growth and development. Other types of soluble fertilizers may include micronutrients like iron or magnesium for specific plant needs. Each type caters to specific nutrient requirements of plants for optimal growth.</p></p>
