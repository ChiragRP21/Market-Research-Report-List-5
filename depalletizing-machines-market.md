<p><p>Depalletizing machines can be categorized into two main types: Semi-automatic and Automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention, such as loading and unloading pallets, while the rest of the process is automated. On the other hand, Automatic machines are fully automated and do not require any manual intervention once the pallet is loaded. Both types of machines offer efficiency and productivity benefits, but Automatic machines are generally preferred for high-volume operations due to their higher level of automation.</p></p>
