<p><p>High performance inertial sensors refer to devices that are capable of accurately measuring the orientation and acceleration of an object. The market for these sensors can be segmented into two main types: angular rate gyros and line accelerometers. Angular rate gyros measure the rate of rotation of an object, while line accelerometers measure linear acceleration along a single axis. Both types are essential for a wide range of applications such as navigation systems, robotics, and aerospace technology.</p></p>
