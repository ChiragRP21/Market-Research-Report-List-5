<p><ul><li>Alumina Slurry</li><li>Colloidal Silica Slurry</li><li>Ceria Slurry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP Polishing Slurry Market comprises three main types: Alumina Slurry, Colloidal Silica Slurry, and Ceria Slurry. Alumina slurry is primarily used for polishing metal surfaces, providing excellent performance and low defectivity. Colloidal silica slurry is used for polishing dielectric materials and offers high removal rates and uniformity. Ceria slurry is commonly used for polishing oxide materials and provides high selectivity and planarity. These different types of slurries cater to various polishing needs in the semiconductor and electronics industries.</p></p>
