<p><p>Collagen salami casings come in two main types: edible collagen casings and non-edible collagen casings. Edible collagen casings are designed to be consumed along with the salami, providing a convenient and flavorful casing for the meat. On the other hand, non-edible collagen casings are meant to be removed before consumption, serving primarily as a convenient and cost-effective alternative to traditional animal-based casings. Both types offer advantages in terms of shelf life, uniformity, and ease of use for salami producers.</p></p>
