<p><p>Marine window wipes market can be categorized into three main types: Straight Line Wipers, Pantograph Wipers, and Pendulum Wipers. Straight Line Wipers move back and forth in a straight line motion to wipe the windows. Pantograph Wipers use a scissor-like mechanism to clean windows with a wider range of motion. Pendulum Wipers swing in an arc motion to effectively wipe windows. Each type offers unique benefits and is designed to meet different needs in the marine industry.</p></p>
