<p><p>Bio-fertilizers are of various types including nitrogen-fixing fertilizers, phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers, potash-mobilizing fertilizers, and others. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers help plants absorb nitrogen from the air, phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers aid in releasing phosphorus from the soil, and potash-mobilizing fertilizers enhance the uptake of potassium. Other types of bio-fertilizers may include those containing beneficial microorganisms or organic substances that improve soil health and plant growth. These different types of bio-fertilizers cater to specific nutrient needs of different crops.</p></p>
