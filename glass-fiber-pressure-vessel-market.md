<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Pressure Vessel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PAN-Based</li><li>Pitch-Based</li><li>Viscose-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass Fiber Pressure Vessels are commonly classified based on the type of raw material used - PAN-Based, Pitch-Based, Viscose-Based. PAN-Based pressure vessels are made from polyacrylonitrile fibers and are most widely used due to their exceptional properties such as high strength and stiffness. Pitch-Based vessels are manufactured from petroleum or coal tar pitch and are known for their excellent thermal and chemical resistance. Viscose-Based vessels are made from cellulose fibers and are preferred for their high impact resistance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
