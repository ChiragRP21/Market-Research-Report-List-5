<p><p>Radio receivers can be classified into different types based on their construction and functionality. The Superhetrodyne Receiver is the most common type, utilizing a complex circuit to convert incoming radio signals to an intermediate frequency for amplification. Reflex Receivers use a single amplifier for both the radio frequency and audio frequency stages, simplifying the circuit design. Other types of radio receivers include Regenerative, Direct Conversion, and Software-defined Radios, each offering unique advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
