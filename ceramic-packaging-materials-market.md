<p><p>Ceramic packaging materials can be categorized into three main types based on their properties and applications. Mechanical ceramic materials are used for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for protecting fragile components. Thermal ceramic materials are chosen for their ability to withstand high temperatures and provide insulation, essential for electronics that generate heat. Electrical ceramic materials are used for their excellent electrical properties, making them suitable for components that require insulation and conductivity. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and performance of packaged electronic devices.</p></p>
