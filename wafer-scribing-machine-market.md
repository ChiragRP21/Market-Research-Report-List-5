<p><p>Wafer scribing machines are used in the semiconductor industry to divide wafers into individual chips. There are two main types of wafer scribing machines on the market: semi-automatic and full-automatic. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention for loading and unloading wafers, while full-automatic machines are capable of operating without human assistance. Full-automatic machines are generally more efficient and can handle higher volumes of wafers, making them ideal for large-scale production operations. Semi-automatic machines, on the other hand, are more suitable for smaller-scale or specialized applications where manual intervention is beneficial.</p></p>
