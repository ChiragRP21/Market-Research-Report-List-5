<p><p>The edible animal fats market can be categorized into three main types based on their physical state: liquid, solid, and semi-solid. Liquid animal fats, such as tallow and lard, are fluid at room temperature. Solid animal fats, like suet and butter, are in solid form at room temperature. Semi-solid animal fats, such as margarine and shortening, have a consistency between liquid and solid forms. Each type of animal fat is utilized in various culinary applications based on its physical properties.</p></p>
