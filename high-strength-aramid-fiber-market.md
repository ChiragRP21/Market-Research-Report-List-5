<p><p>High strength aramid fiber market comprises two main types: Meta-Aramid and Para-Aramid. Meta-Aramid fibers are known for their excellent heat resistance and flame-retardant properties, making them ideal for use in protective clothing and industrial applications. On the other hand, Para-Aramid fibers are valued for their high tensile strength and resistance to abrasion, making them suitable for use in aerospace, automotive, and ballistic protection applications.
