<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Semi-automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manual bottle case packers require operators to manually load bottles into cases. These are ideal for small-scale operations with low production volumes. Semi-automatic packers automate some of the processes, such as case erection and bottle loading, but still require some manual intervention. These are suitable for medium-scale operations. Fully automatic packers are highly automated, requiring minimal human intervention. They are ideal for large-scale operations with high production volumes. Each type of packer offers different levels of efficiency, productivity, and labor requirements.</p></p>
