There are three main types of navigation systems: Optical, Electromagnetic (EM), and Hybrid. Optical systems use cameras and markers to track instrument movements, EM systems use magnetic fields for tracking, and Hybrid systems combine both optical and EM technologies. Each type has its own set of advantages and limitations, allowing dentists to choose the system that best suits their needs and preferences.</p></p>
