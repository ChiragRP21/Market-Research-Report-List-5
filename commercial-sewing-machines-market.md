<p><p>Commercial sewing machines are divided into two main types: manual sewing machines and electric sewing machines. Manual sewing machines require the operator to use a hand crank to control the stitching process, making them more labor-intensive but also more affordable. On the other hand, electric sewing machines are more efficient and easier to use, as they are powered by electricity and have built-in motors to control the stitching process. Both types cater to different needs and budget constraints in the commercial sewing industry.</p></p>
