<p><p>Veneers are dental materials used to enhance the appearance of teeth by covering imperfections. There are two main types of veneers on the market: composite material and dental porcelain. Composite veneers are made of a resin material that is directly applied to the teeth and shaped by a dentist. Dental porcelain veneers are thin shells of ceramic material that are custom-made in a dental lab and bonded to the teeth. Both types of veneers can improve the color, shape, and alignment of teeth for a more aesthetically pleasing smile.</p></p>
