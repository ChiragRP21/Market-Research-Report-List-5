<p><p>Infrared light sensors can be categorized into two main types: contact light sensors and non-contact light sensors. Contact light sensors require physical contact with the object or surface being measured, while non-contact light sensors can detect light and temperature without direct contact. Contact light sensors are commonly used in industrial applications where precise measurements are needed, while non-contact light sensors are more commonly found in consumer electronics and security systems. Both types of sensors play an important role in detecting and measuring light levels in various applications.</p></p>
