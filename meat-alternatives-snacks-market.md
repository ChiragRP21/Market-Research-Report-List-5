<p><p>Meat alternative snacks come in various types including Soy-Based, Wheat-Based, Mycoprotein, and Others. Soy-based snacks are made from soy protein and mimic the texture and taste of meat. Wheat-based snacks are made from wheat gluten and provide a chewy texture similar to meat. Mycoprotein snacks use fungi as the main ingredient, offering a meat-like consistency. Other meat alternative snacks may include products made from legumes, vegetables, or grains to provide a plant-based option for those looking to reduce their meat consumption.</p></p>
