EEG measures the electrical activity in the brain, EMG measures the electrical activity in muscles, Transcranial Doppler monitors blood flow in the brain, Intracranial Pressure Monitor measures pressure inside the skull, and Cerebral Oximeters measure oxygen levels in the brain. These devices are crucial for diagnosing and monitoring neurological conditions and brain injuries.</p></p>
