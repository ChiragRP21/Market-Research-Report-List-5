<p><p>A Lung Isolation Device is used in medical procedures to block air to one lung while the other lung is being operated on. There are two main types of Lung Isolation Devices - Endobronchial Blockers and Lung Dissociation Kits. Endobronchial Blockers are inserted through a bronchoscope and inflated to block the bronchus. Lung Dissociation Kits are used to separate the lungs for independent ventilation. Both devices allow for more controlled and precise surgeries on the lung.</p></p>
