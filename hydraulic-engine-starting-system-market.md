<p><p>The hydraulic engine starting system market includes two main types: geared hydraulic starters and piston hydraulic starters. Geared hydraulic starters use a gear arrangement to transmit power from the hydraulic motor to the engine flywheel, providing a reliable and efficient starting solution. On the other hand, piston hydraulic starters utilize a piston-cylinder arrangement to generate the force needed to start the engine. Both types of hydraulic starters offer reliable starting performance, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
