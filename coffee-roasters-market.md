<p><p>Direct-fire coffee roasters use direct heat through a burner to roast coffee beans, resulting in a traditional and robust flavor. Semi-direct fire with half hot air roasters combine direct heat with hot air circulation to provide a more even roast. Hot-air roasters use solely hot air to roast beans, resulting in a cleaner taste profile. Other types of roasters may include barrel, drum, or fluid bed roasters, each offering unique advantages in terms of flavor, efficiency, and versatility in the roasting process.</p></p>
