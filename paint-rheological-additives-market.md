<p><strong>The Paint Rheological Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inorganic Type</li><li>Organic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paint rheological additives market can be categorized into inorganic type and organic type. Inorganic additives include clay, silica, and fumed silica, which are widely used for their thixotropic and anti-sagging properties. Organic additives, on the other hand, are based on polymers like cellulose ethers, polyurethanes, and acrylics, known for their shear-thinning and thickening properties. Both types of additives contribute to controlling the flow behavior, leveling, and anti-settling properties of paint formulations, offering a wide range of options for formulators to tailor the rheology of paints for different applications.</p></p>
