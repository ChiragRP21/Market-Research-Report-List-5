<p><p>Aluminum foam for construction is categorized into two main types: open cell aluminum foam and closed cell aluminum foam. Open cell aluminum foam is porous and allows for the passage of air, making it lightweight and thermally insulating, while closed cell aluminum foam is non-porous and has a higher strength-to-weight ratio, making it more rigid and suitable for structural applications. Both types of aluminum foam offer unique properties that make them ideal for use in various construction projects.</p></p>
