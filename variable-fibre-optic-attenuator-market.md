<p><p>Variable Fibre Optic Attenuators come in two main types - manual and electrical. Manual Variable Fibre Optic Attenuators require physical adjustment by twisting or sliding the device to control the amount of light passing through. On the other hand, Electrical Variable Fibre Optic Attenuators use electronic components to adjust the attenuation levels, offering more precise control and automation options. Both types serve the same purpose of regulating the intensity of light signals in fibre optic communication systems.</p></p>
