<p><p>Solvent-based Packaging Adhesive comes in two main types: one-component and two-component. One-component adhesives are ready to use and do not require any mixing before application. This makes them convenient and easy to use in packaging applications. On the other hand, two-component adhesives require mixing of two different components before application. This allows for better customization and control over the adhesive properties, making them ideal for more specialized packaging requirements. Both types offer strong bonding capabilities for a variety of packaging materials.</p></p>
