<p><p>Cardiovascular Information Systems (CVIS) can be categorized into three main types: On-Site CVIS, Web-Based CVIS, and Cloud-Based CVIS. On-Site CVIS involves hosting the system within the healthcare facility's infrastructure. Web-Based CVIS allows users to access the system remotely through a web browser. Cloud-Based CVIS involves storing and accessing data through a cloud service provider. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of accessibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness for managing cardiovascular information within healthcare organizations.</p></p>
