<p><p>Plasma Surface Preparation Machines are categorized into two main types: Atmospheric Pressure Plasma Surface Preparation Machines and Low Pressure/Vacuum Plasma Surface Preparation Machines. Atmospheric Pressure Plasma machines operate at regular atmospheric pressure, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications. On the other hand, Low Pressure/Vacuum Plasma machines operate at reduced pressures, providing a more controlled environment for delicate surface treatments.
