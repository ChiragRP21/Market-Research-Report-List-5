<p><p>Tufted carpet tile market offers three main types based on pile construction: cut pile, loop pile, and cut and loop pile. Cut pile carpets have individual yarns cut to an even length, providing a soft and plush appearance. Loop pile carpets feature continuous loops of yarn for a durable and textured finish. Cut and loop pile carpets combine both cut and looped yarns to create a variety of patterns and textures. Each type offers unique characteristics to cater to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
