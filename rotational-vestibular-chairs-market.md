<p><p>Rotational vestibular chairs are designed to provide various types of rotational movements for therapeutic purposes. Manual wholebody rotation chairs require the user to manually rotate their entire body. Passive head-on-body rotation chairs move the user's head in relation to their body without any effort on the user's part. Active head-on-body rotation chairs require the user to actively move their head in relation to their body. Each type of chair offers unique benefits for treating vestibular disorders and improving balance and coordination.</p></p>
