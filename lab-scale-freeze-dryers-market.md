<p><p>Lab-scale freeze dryers are available in three main types based on condenser temperature: -55℃, -85℃, and -105℃. The type of freeze dryer chosen depends on the specific requirements of the product being dried. A condenser temperature of -55℃ is suitable for less heat-sensitive products, while a condenser temperature of -85℃ is ideal for preserving biological samples. A condenser temperature of -105℃ is used for highly heat-sensitive materials, such as pharmaceuticals and vaccines. Each type offers unique benefits for different applications.</p></p>
