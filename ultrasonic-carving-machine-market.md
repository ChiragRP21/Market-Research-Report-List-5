<p><p>Ultrasonic carving machines can be divided into three main types based on their control systems: CNC, manually-controlled, and PLC-controlled. CNC carving machines are computer-programmed for precise and automated carving processes, making them ideal for complex designs. Manually-controlled machines require operators to control the carving process using physical mechanisms. PLC-controlled machines utilize programmable logic controllers to automate the carving process while providing flexibility for customizing carving parameters. Each type of machine offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications in the ultrasonic carving market.</p></p>
