<p><p>Compressed Air Foam System (CAFS) is a firefighting technology that mixes compressed air with foam concentrate and water to create a foam solution. The system is available in three types: low-expansion foam system, which produces thick foam with minimal expansion ratio; medium-expansion foam system, which generates foam with a moderate expansion ratio; and high-expansion foam system, which produces large amounts of foam with a high expansion ratio. Each type is used in different firefighting scenarios based on the level of expansion needed.</p></p>
