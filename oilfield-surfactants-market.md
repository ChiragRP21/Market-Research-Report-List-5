<p><p>Oilfield surfactants market can be categorized into four types: Anionic Surfactants, which have a negatively charged hydrophilic head; Non-Ionic Surfactants, which do not have a charge and are used for reducing interfacial tension; Cationic Surfactants, with a positively charged head group, are used for emulsification and corrosion inhibition; Amphoteric Surfactants, have both positive and negative charged groups and are versatile in their application. Each type of surfactant plays a unique role in enhancing oil recovery and production in the oilfield industry.</p></p>
