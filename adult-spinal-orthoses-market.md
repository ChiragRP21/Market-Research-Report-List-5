<p><p>Adult Spinal Orthoses Market includes Dynamic Orthotics and Static Orthotics. Dynamic Orthotics are designed to provide dynamic support and mobilization to the spine, allowing for controlled movement and flexibility. On the other hand, Static Orthotics are rigid braces that restrict movement and provide stability to the spine. Dynamic orthotics are often used for conditions requiring both support and mobility, while static orthotics are commonly used for conditions where immobilization is necessary to promote healing. These two types cater to different needs in the adult spinal orthoses market.</p></p>
