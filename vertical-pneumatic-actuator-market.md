<p><strong>The Vertical Pneumatic Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-acting Actuators</li><li>Double-acting Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vertical Pneumatic Actuator market consists of two main types: Single-acting Actuators and Double-acting Actuators. Single-acting actuators use air pressure to extend the actuator and a spring to retract it, making them ideal for applications where a force is only needed in one direction. On the other hand, Double-acting actuators use air pressure to extend and retract the actuator, providing a more versatile solution for applications requiring force in both directions. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
