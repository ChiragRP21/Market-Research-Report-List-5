<p><strong>The Soy Protein Concentrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous Alcohol Washing Process Product</li><li>Acid Washing Process Product</li><li>Heat Denaturation Process Product</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soy protein concentrates are primarily categorized into three types based on the manufacturing processes used: Aqueous Alcohol Washing Process Product, Acid Washing Process Product, and Heat Denaturation Process Product. The Aqueous Alcohol Washing Process involves extracting protein using alcohol and water, resulting in a high protein content product. Acid Washing Process involves treating soy flakes with acid to remove fiber and carbohydrates, leaving behind a protein-rich concentrate. Heat Denaturation Process utilizes heat to denature proteins, resulting in a product with improved solubility and functionality.</p></p>
