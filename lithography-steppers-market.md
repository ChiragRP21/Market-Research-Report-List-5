<p><strong>The Lithography Steppers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV</li><li>DUV</li><li>EUV</li><li>Nanoimprint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithography steppers are used in the production of semiconductor devices, with various types available based on the wavelength of light used for patterning. UV steppers use ultraviolet light, DUV steppers use deep ultraviolet light, and EUV steppers use extreme ultraviolet light for finer patterning. Nanoimprint lithography utilizes a different technique by physically pressing a pattern into a substrate. Each of these markets cater to different requirements for semiconductor manufacturers in terms of resolution, throughput, and cost.</p></p>
