<p><p>Neuro Stimulation Equipment Market includes two main types: Implantable Devices and External Devices. Implantable devices are surgically implanted into the body to deliver electrical impulses directly to the targeted nerves or brain areas, while external devices are worn outside the body and deliver stimulation through electrodes placed on the skin. Implantable devices offer a more precise and continuous stimulation, suitable for chronic conditions, while external devices are more non-invasive and often used for temporary relief or rehabilitation purposes.</p></p>
