<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal</li><li>Optical Network Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GPON equipment market consists of two main types: Optical Line Terminal (OLT) and Optical Network Terminal (ONT). OLT serves as the aggregator and distributor of the data signals within the GPON network, while ONT is the endpoint that connects to the user's devices such as computers and telephones. OLTs are typically located in data centers or central offices, while ONTs are installed at the customers' premises. Both play crucial roles in delivering high-speed internet and other services over fiber optic networks.</p></p>
