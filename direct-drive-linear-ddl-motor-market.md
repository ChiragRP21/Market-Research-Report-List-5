<p><ul><li>Ironcore Linear Motors</li><li>Ironless Linear Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct Drive Linear (DDL) Motors come in two main types: Ironcore Linear Motors and Ironless Linear Motors. Ironcore Linear Motors use a traditional design with a movable coil winding inside a stationary iron core, providing strong forces and high precision. On the other hand, Ironless Linear Motors eliminate the iron core, allowing for smoother operation, faster acceleration, and higher speeds. Each type caters to different applications and requirements, offering unique advantages and performance characteristics in the DDL motor market.</p></p>
