<p><p>Soil stabilization materials market is categorized into three main types: Polymers, Minerals, and Stabilizing Agents. Polymers are synthetic materials that improve soil strength and durability. Minerals like lime, cement, and fly ash react with soil particles to increase stability. Stabilizing agents are organic materials that bind soil particles together, preventing erosion. Each type of material offers unique benefits for stabilizing soil in different applications, such as construction, agriculture, and landscaping.</p></p>
