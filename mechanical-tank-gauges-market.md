<p><strong>The Mechanical Tank Gauges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubular Type</li><li>Reflection Type</li><li>Transparent Type</li><li>Bicolor Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical tank gauges come in various types such as tubular, reflection, transparent, bicolor, and others. Tubular gauges are simple and cost-effective, using a glass tube to visually measure liquid levels. Reflection gauges use light to determine liquid levels and can be suitable for corrosive liquids. Transparent gauges provide direct visibility into the tank. Bicolor gauges have colored bands to indicate liquid levels. Other types may include magnetic gauges or float-operated gauges for specific applications. Each type offers unique features and benefits for measuring tank levels.</p></p>
