<p><p>Antimicrobial coating services involve applying coatings with organic antimicrobial additives or antimicrobial metals as additives. Coating with organic antimicrobial additives typically involves incorporating substances like silver ions or copper nanoparticles into the coating to inhibit the growth of bacteria and other microbes. On the other hand, antimicrobial metals as additives for coatings involve directly adding metals such as silver, copper, or zinc to the coating, which also have antimicrobial properties. Both types of coatings help prevent the growth and spread of harmful microbes on surfaces.</p></p>
