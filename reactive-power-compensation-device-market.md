<p><p>Reactive Power Compensation Devices, such as Static Var Compensators (SVC) and Static Var Generators (SVG), are essential for maintaining power system stability and voltage regulation. SVC dynamically controls the reactive power by varying the impedance and absorbing or supplying reactive power as needed. SVG, on the other hand, generates or absorbs reactive power using power electronics. Both devices are crucial in improving power quality, reducing transmission losses, and enhancing grid reliability. They play a vital role in ensuring a stable and efficient power supply system.</p></p>
