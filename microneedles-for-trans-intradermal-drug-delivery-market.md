<p><p>Microneedles for transdermal and intradermal drug delivery come in three main types - solid microneedles, hollow microneedles, and dissolvable microneedles. Solid microneedles are made of a solid material and are best for delivering drugs that do not require precise dosage control. Hollow microneedles have a hollow center through which drug formulations can be directly injected into the skin. Dissolvable microneedles are made of materials that dissolve upon insertion, delivering the drug into the skin before dissipating. Each type offers unique advantages for drug delivery applications.</p></p>
