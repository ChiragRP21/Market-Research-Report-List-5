<p><p>3-Terminal capacitors are available in three main market types: Radial, SMD (Surface Mount Device), and SMT (Surface Mount Technology). </p><p>Radial capacitors have two leads that extend out from the same side of the component, making them easy to insert through holes on a printed circuit board. </p><p>SMD capacitors are compact and are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of the PCB, saving space and allowing for automated assembly.</p><p>SMT capacitors are similar to SMD but are specifically designed for use in surface mount technology applications.</p></p>
