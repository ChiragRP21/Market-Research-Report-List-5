<p><p>The Ultrapure Water Resin Market includes three main types: Mixed Resins, Anion Resins, and Cationic Resins. Mixed resins are a combination of anion and cation resins and are commonly used for general water treatment applications. Anion resins selectively remove negatively charged ions from water, while cationic resins remove positively charged ions. Each type of resin plays a specific role in the purification process, ensuring that ultrapure water is free from contaminants and suitable for various industrial and laboratory applications.</p></p>
