<p><ul><li>Rigid Packaging</li><li>Flexible Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fresh seafood packaging includes two main types: rigid packaging and flexible packaging. Rigid packaging is commonly used for seafood products that need extra protection and support, such as frozen seafood or delicate fish filets. It is made of durable materials like plastic or glass. On the other hand, flexible packaging is more commonly used for fresh seafood products like shrimp or scallops. It is made of materials like plastic film or foil that can easily conform to the shape of the seafood. Both types of packaging are essential in ensuring the freshness and quality of seafood products.</p></p>
