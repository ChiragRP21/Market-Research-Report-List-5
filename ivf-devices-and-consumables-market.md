<p><p>IVF devices and consumables market can be classified into three main types: Fresh Embryo IVF, Frozen Embryo IVF, and Donor Egg IVF. Fresh Embryo IVF involves fertilizing an egg with sperm and transferring the resulting embryo into the uterus on the same day. Frozen Embryo IVF involves storing embryos for later use. Donor Egg IVF uses eggs from a donor to be fertilized with sperm and transferred into the recipient's uterus. Each type offers unique advantages and challenges for couples seeking fertility treatment.</p></p>
