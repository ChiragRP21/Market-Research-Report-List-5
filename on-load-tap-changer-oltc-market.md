<p><p>On-load Tap Changers (OLTC) are devices used in power transformers to adjust the voltage ratio while the transformer is energized. There are two main types of OLTC on the market: Vacuum OLTC and Oil-immersed OLTC. Vacuum OLTCs use vacuum interrupters to make and break the circuit during tap changing operations, while Oil-immersed OLTCs use oil as an insulating medium. Both types offer advantages and disadvantages depending on the specific application and requirements of the transformer system.</p></p>
