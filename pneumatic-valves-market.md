<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pneumatic Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Way</li><li>Three-Way</li><li>Four-Way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic valves are classified into different types based on the number of ports they have. Two-way valves have two ports, one for air intake and one for air exhaust. They are commonly used for on-off applications. Three-way valves have three ports, allowing for either exhaust or pressure release. They are often used for simple automation tasks. Four-way valves have four ports, enabling pressure to be alternated between two different paths. They are commonly used in more complex pneumatic systems for controlling the direction of air flow.</p></p>
