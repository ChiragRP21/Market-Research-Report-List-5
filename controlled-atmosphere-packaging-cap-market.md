<p><p>Controlled Atmosphere Packaging (CAP) involves modifying the atmosphere surrounding a product to extend its shelf life. Different types of materials are used in CAP, including Polyvinylidene chloride (PVDC), Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH), Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS), and other materials. PVDC provides excellent barrier properties, EVOH is a high barrier material, ABS is known for its toughness and resistance to chemicals. Other materials used in CAP may include aluminum foil, nylon, and polyethylene. Each type of material offers unique properties to enhance the effectiveness of controlled atmosphere packaging.</p></p>
